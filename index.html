General Garden
Services

My name is Ben and I am happy to help you in your garden

I undertake mowing, pruning, hedge cutting, reductions, clear outs, weeding, lawn care, general maintenance, pressure washing, gutter clearing, leaf collection, and other seasonal or one-off garden duties.

Lawn Care

I’ll mow your lawn, trim those tricky spots, and edge the borders for a neat finish. New edges can also be dug in, and I’ll clear any debris with a blower to leave your garden spotless. Scarification services are also available to keep your lawn healthy and lush.

Pruning

Tame those overgrown shrubs! Restore shape and height to bring them back to their best. By lifting the lower branches and revealing the soil beneath, we make it easier to manage weeds and keep your garden looking clean and cared for.

Hedge
Cutting

I can keep your hedges looking sharp and healthy. I work with all types — from formal box hedging to fast-growing leylandii — and always leave your space tidy and well-presented.

Pruning
and Reductions

I offer expert pruning and reduction work. From seasonal trims to more substantial cutbacks, I ensure your shrubs, trees, and hedges stay healthy, balanced, and looking their best.

Clear
Outs

Overgrown, cluttered or just in need of a fresh start? Whether it’s an untamed garden or a space that’s become hard to manage, I provide full garden clear outs. I’ll remove unwanted growth, tidy borders, and restore order, leaving you with a clean slate and a garden that’s ready to shine again.

General
Maintenance

Keeping on top of things makes all the difference. Regular mowing to keep your lawn neat and tidy, along with weeding to stop unwanted growth from taking over. A straightforward, reliable service to keep your garden looking its best all season long.
