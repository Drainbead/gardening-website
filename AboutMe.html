My Gardening
Experience

I'm a passionate gardener based in Peterborough with over six years of hands-on experience. My journey began working with established firms, where I mastered the art of precise lawn care and professional standards. I later had the unique opportunity to maintain high-end private gardens, developing an eye for detail and an appreciation for tailored, client-focused service. Most recently, I worked alongside a seasoned self-employed gardener who helped refine my approach to truly understanding what each customer wants from their outdoor space — not just maintaining it, but helping it thrive. I now run BCA Gardening Service as a sole trader, offering reliable, skilled, and friendly garden care. I bring my own van, professional tools, and a can-do attitude to every job. I'm observant, motivated, and easy to work with — and I treat every garden as if it were my own. Whether you're after regular maintenance, a one-off tidy, or just want your space to feel more loved again, I'm here to help.
